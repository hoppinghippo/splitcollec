A cloud based platform for water, gas, and electric utiltiy companies

What is Split Collective

Split Collective is a cloud based
solution that helps lower cost, improve
the environment and enable
utility companies (electric, gas, water) better collaboration and communication with their centennial customers.

Where will Split Collective be used?

Utility companies can integrate their branding to deliver the split collective portal to their customer via mobile app or web site. Split Collective can be used for residential and commercial locations.

Who is Split Collective for?

The Split Collective solution will enable a deeper understanding of utility costs and how that translates to everyday life. It will provide dialogue responsive to Centennials’ needs on an ongoing basis, eliminate the sporadic communication of traditional mail, and enable them to feel more power in collaborating their energy future. Split Collective ensure that there will not be a loss of trust between the utility institutions and their consumer base, as Centennials make up more of the paying population.

The Cententials

Centennials are a hybrid class comprised of those born at the tail-end of the Millennial generation (1992-2001) and those in Generation Z (2003-2015). Centennials are the first generation of lifelong digital natives; constantly connected by laptops, mobile phones, and e-tablets since birth, web- and telecommunications technology are as ubiquitous to Centennials as socks and shoes.

Split Collective is an effective solution for Centennials

Why the cententials?

Therefore, utility companies must adopt daily, data-driven dialogue and seek to collaborate with Centennials. They want to know that their utility providers are conscious of their values and interests and that they will be offered innovative ways to control energy costs while simultaneously being respectful of the natural environment.

What does this mean?

Research on Centennials conducted by The Futures Company in 2015 showed that centennials are extremely distrustful and impatient with of large institutions. Subpar service not be accepted; and without hesitation or warning, Centennials will abandon institutions that are not responding to their needs or concerns quickly enough. Instead, they will believe they can leverage advances in technology to create their own institutions that provide services more personalized to their needs.

How do we address this?

The Split Collective solution enables users to gain a deeper understanding of their utility consumption habits, the corresponding costs, and how smarter practices can fit easily into everyday life. It is designed to inform and respond to Centennials’ needs and concerns continuously; and thereby eliminate the sporadic traditional mailings, which most people reflexively ignore.

Features

Updates

Real time updates through their phones or tablets which indicate how much they spend when they turn on a TV or adjust the temperature.

Tips

Users receive helpful tips on how to reduce their bill, maximizing their savings.

Usage

In homes with multiple tenants, roommates will be able to pay for just their specific share of the home’s energy usage

Sharing

Low income residents receive increases in utility allowances by showing personal career progress.

Alternatives

The ability to communicate and purchase new clean energy infrastructure alternatives to help drive down the price.

Goals

Users can set their own energy usage benchmarks based on their own lifestyle or budget.
