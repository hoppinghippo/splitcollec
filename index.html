MISSION

Split Collective is a cloud based
solution that helps lower cost, improve
the environment and enable
utility companies (electric, gas, water) better collaboration and communication with their centennial customers.

BENEFITS

Advice

Users receive helpful tips on how to reduce their bill, maximizing their bottom line.

Usage

In homes with multiple tenants, roommates will be able to pay for just their specific share of the energy usage.

Split Bill Pay

Users can now split up their bill based on individual usage.

Updates

Real-time daily updates through phone or tablet to understand how much they spend when they turn on a TV or adjust the temperature.

Goals

Users can set
their own energy usage benchmarks based on their life and budget.

Sharing

Low income residents can receive increases in utility amounts by showing social progress.

CHARACTERISTICS

Who are the cententials?

      Centennials are a hybrid class comprised of those born at the tail-end of the Millennial generation (1992-2001) and those in Generation Z (2003-2015). Centennials are the first generation of lifelong digital natives; constantly connected by laptops, mobile phones, and e-tablets since birth, web- and telecommunications technology are as ubiquitous to Centennials as socks and shoes.

What does this mean?

      Research conducted by The Futures Company in their 2015 study on Centennials shows centennials are extremely impatient with regards to institutions. Subpar service will no longer be accepted, without hesitation or warning Centennials will abandon institutions that aren’t responding to their needs quick enough. They will expect to leverage advances in technology to strike out and create their own institutions that provide service more personalized to their needs

Why is it important?

      The centenials are estimated to make up over 75% of the global workforce by 2025. Therefore it is imperative that utility companies adopt daily data-driven dialogue, and collaboration with this population. It’s important to establish that utilities are conscious of their costs and trying to find innovative ways to provide savings while being respectful of the environment.
